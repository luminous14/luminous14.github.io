What is pair-programming?

Pair programming is a methodology whereby two or more individuals simultaneosly collaborate on writing software. Each alternates between the role of Driver and Navigator.

Why is pair-programming important to DBC?

Pair programming is important to DBC to ensure that boots develop the skills they need to work on projects in the real world. Pairing has also demonstrated better outcomes than individual effort.
